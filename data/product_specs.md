# Product Specification: OmniBot 3000

## Overview
The OmniBot 3000 is our flagship AI-powered vacuum cleaner designed for industrial warehouses.

## Technical Specs
- **Battery Life:** 12 hours of continuous operation.
- **Charging Time:** 2 hours for a full charge.
- **Sensors:** LiDAR, Ultrasonic, and Infrared for obstacle avoidance.
- **Capacity:** 5-liter dust bin.

## Maintenance
The HEPA filter should be replaced every 6 months for optimal performance.
