# Human Resources & Remote Work Policy

## Remote Work
Employees are eligible for a hybrid work schedule. You are required to be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Monday, Wednesday, and Friday are optional remote days.

## Annual Leave
Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. Leave requests must be submitted via the HR Portal at least two weeks in advance.

## Dress Code
The office follows a "Business Casual" dress code. Fridays are "Casual Fridays" where jeans are permitted.
