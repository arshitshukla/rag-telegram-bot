# Office Facilities & Kitchen Rules

## Location
Our main headquarters is located at 123 Innovation Drive, Suite 500, San Francisco, CA. 

## Kitchen Etiquette
- Please label all food kept in the communal refrigerator with your name and date.
- Every Friday at 4:00 PM, the fridge is cleared. Any unlabeled items will be discarded.
- The espresso machine requires cleaning after every 10 uses. Instructions are posted on the cabinet above the machine.

## Parking
Employees can validate their parking stubs at the front desk for a 50% discount on daily rates.
